Chronicle Covers

The San Francisco Chronicle came into existence more than 150 years ago as The Daily Dramatic Chronicle, “a daily record of affairs — local, critical and theatrical.” Chronicle Covers is a yearlong project that will highlight one classic Chronicle newspaper page from our archive every day for 366 days.
